RoxAnn's Hobbies

Patterns

Rox enjoys making things for the seasons, holidays, and birthdays. She finds many of her ideas on sites such as Pintrest™, Etsy™, and LoveSVG™. She also uses Cricut Access™ and them cuts her patterns out with the Cricut™ machine.

Wood Working

Rox also enjoys working with wood. She collects scrap lumber from her brothers' and husband's projects. She then uses a jigsaw tool or a table saw to create the desired shape for her ideas.

Volleyball

RoxAnn is a Husker fan without a doubt. She is an ardent follower of the Lady Huskers Volleyball team and attends their games whenever she has the time. She also enjoys Husker Football and the Colorado Rockies.
